◆ closedPolygonList

vector<vector<int> > QMesh::MeshData::closedPolygonList

The polygon collection containing the index collection of the polygons. Closed polygons are important to define polygon colliders of the mesh. The particle orders should be clockwise. The integer values define the indices of particles in the particlePositions collection.

◆ internalSpringList

vector<pair<int,int> > QMesh::MeshData::internalSpringList

The collection of integer pairs to define internal springs. Internal springs are important for the some mass spring simulation configrations. The integer values define the indices of particles in the particlePositions collection.
